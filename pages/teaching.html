Teaching

I prioritize using research-based best practices to teach physics. At Pacific, we use the Workshop Physics curriculum, which emphasizes hands on active learning as much as possible. The image below shows me moderating while a group of students discusses a problem using a whiteboard to draw their ideas.

Learning is a personal experience, the need to know something is often linked to the big click we feel in gaining new knowledge. It helps if you can see, or act out the concept you are trying to learn. The best lessons are the hard ones, you remember when you struggle.
